Welcome to Chaturya school of Kalashastra

where the timeless beauty of Indian arts and aesthetics comes alive. Our programs offer an immersive journey into the 64 Kalas, exploring traditional skills that define India's artistic heritage. Dive into Natya and Sangita Shastra, where you'll uncover the foundations of dance, drama, performance arts, music theory, and the intricacies of traditional instruments, celebrating the performing arts in all their depth and vibrancy. Our Chitra Kala program delves into visual arts, embracing painting, sculpture, and various forms of artistic expression that capture the spirit of cultural storytelling.

At Chaturya, we aim to cultivate not only artistic skills but also an appreciation for the philosophies and histories that enrich each art form. Join us in exploring this profound tapestry of Indian art, where tradition meets creative expression and ancient wisdom inspires modern artistry.
