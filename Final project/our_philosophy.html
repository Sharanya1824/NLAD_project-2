History of Watches

The first milestone of watch started in the 16th century in Europe.Peter Henlein, a German watchmaker from Nuremberg was the inventor of first watch "Watch 1505" but they were designed to remain in pocket than on wrists.They were called 'Nuremberg eggs' since they were like eggs as they became flatter they were named 'Gusset watches'.

Evolution of Wristwatches

1810

The first wristwatch:the Breguet watch number 2639

The first wristwatch was invented by Abraham-Louis Breguet for the Queen of Naples,Caroline Murat in 1810.Unfortunetly there is no picture or sketches of the watch since it was lost in 1855.

1868

Caliber 27368

This was designed for Countess Koscewicz of Hungary by swiss manufacturer Patek Phillipe.

1904

Cartier Santos-Dumont

The Santos was the first practical wristwatch made and designed by Louis Cartier.

1957

Hamilton 500

Hamilton 500 was first electrically powered watch invented by Hamilton watch company in Pennsylvania.

1976

Pulsar LED

Pulsar LED was the first digital watch developed by Hamilton and Electro/Data inc.

1990

Receptor

Receptor was world's first wristwatch with a pager function,allowing users to receieve alphnumeric messages on their wrist developed by Seiko

2006

Sony Ericsson MBW100

Sony Ericsson MBW100 was the smartwatch with full touchscreen and internet browsing capabilities developed by Sony.

2022

Vivomove Trend

Vivomove trend is a unique design with analog hands dynamically moving out of the way when interacting with the touchscreen developed by Garmin.

Oyster was the first waterproof watch developed by Rolex in 1926.

Astron 35SQ was the first watch with Quartz technology developed by Seiko in 1958.

DiaStar 1 was the first scratchproof watch developed by Rado in 1962 using tungsten alloy

Trench watches were developed by Rolex from pocket watches to solve the problems faced during world war I.
