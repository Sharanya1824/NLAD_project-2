Library

Digital & Physical Resources

The Chaturya Library is a knowledge haven, housing an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources. With a focus on interdisciplinary studies, it provides access to thousands of e-books, research papers, and industry reports. The library also collaborates with global databases to ensure that students and faculty have the latest resources at their fingertips.

Research and Study Hubs

To foster in-depth research, the library includes dedicated spaces for quiet study, group discussions, and project collaboration. The Research Commons offers specialized resources and data analysis tools, empowering students and faculty to work on innovative projects with the highest level of support.

Community Learning Programs

The library at Chaturya offers regular workshops, research seminars, and guest lectures as part of its Community Learning Programs. These initiatives are designed to inspire curiosity, promote lifelong learning, and encourage knowledge sharing across the academic community.
